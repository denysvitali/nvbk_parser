OEMNVBK format notes (fully reverse-engineered 2026-07-08)
===========================================================

See docs/format.md for the authoritative layout.
See docs/re-liboemnvbk-helper.md for evidence from liboemnvbk_img_helper.so.
See docs/re-samples-decode.md for per-sample coverage.

HEADER (0x200 = 512 bytes)
--------------------------
0x00  8   magic "OEMNVBK\0"
0x08  4   version (STA often 01 00 01 01; DYC 01 00 01 00)
0x0c  1   sub_file_count
0x0d  4   table_offset (always 0x1c in samples; unaligned u32)
0x11  1   header_flag (0x00 older, 0x01 OP8-era, 0x02 newer oplus)
0x12  3   build_time YY MM DD (binary, not BCD)
0x15  1   reserved (0)
0x16  6   signature_or_reserved (usually 0; oplus has ASCII tag)
0x1c  …   descriptor table starts here when table_offset==0x1c

There is NO global Total/Valid field in the header.

DESCRIPTOR (0x29 = 41 bytes each)
---------------------------------
0x00  4   record_count (u32 LE)  *** not a single-byte hint ***
0x04  2   start_sector (payload at start_sector * 512)
0x06  2   num_sectors  (length = num_sectors * 512)
0x08 32   SHA-256 of full payload including trailing zero pad
0x28  1   rf_id (0xff = manifest/common; else hw/rf variant)

PAYLOAD = contiguous TLV records (count == record_count) + zero pad
-------------------------------------------------------------------
u32 total | u8 kind | u8 attr | u8 rfid | u8 flags | …

kind 0x01 / 0xF1 / 0xF3 : numeric item  [u16 id][u16 dataLen][data]
kind 0x02 / 0xF2 / 0xF4 : path entry    [u16 1][u16 pathLen][path][u16 2][u16 dlen][data]

VTNV + zlib (78 9c) may wrap large RF payloads inside item data.
